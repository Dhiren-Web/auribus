About Us

Auribus Global Inc. is a Revenue Cycle Management (RCM) service provider committed to making revenue and patient management more measurable and simpler. Every efficient service provider needs back-office services, especially when it comes to the medical provision and healthcare sector. The RCM of a healthcare provider includes medical billing, denial management of claims, AR follow up, concierge, etc. Auribus Global aims to tap into these areas and provide optimum services, so you can focus on your area of expertise. RCM is the management of income that is generated from services rendered by healthcare providers. The process includes all of the procedural steps – right from the making of the patient’s appointment to the final collection of payment. Medical billing is complicated and time consuming, but at Auribus Global, our experts will make it a hassle-free and seamless experience for you.

Our Strength

We are well aware of the commitment, devotion and responsibility with which a healthcare provider treats their patients, but we are also aware of the general disinterest that exists in managing medical billing related activities and processes.


One size does not fit all – and we know this. Therefore , we also provide specially customized services and packages at reliable rates that suit your set of requirements. We believe firmly in understanding our clients first, so that we can custom design a fulfilling and worthwhile just for them
